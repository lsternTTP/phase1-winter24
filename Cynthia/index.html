Zucchini Bread

Ingredients

Takes about 15-20 minutes prep time and bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

1-1/2 cups all purpose flour

1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1/4 teaspoons salt

1 teaspoon ground ginger

1 cup granulated sugar or light baking sugar

1/4 cup vegetable oil

1 cup of shreadded fresh zucchini squash

grated peel and juice of 1 lemon or lime

2 large eggs

Directions

Preheat oven and lightly grease and flour a loaf pan, setting it aside. Combine the dry ingredients (flour, ginger, baking powder, salt and sugar) and mix well. Add oil, eggs, lemon or lime peel, lemon or lime juice and shredded zucchini. Stir only to combine; don't overmix and batter should be smooth. DO NOT ADD THE ZUCCHINI JUICE!

Pour into prepared pan and bake for 50-60 minutes; knife or skewer should come out clean. (NOTE: No need to peel the zucchini, just trim the ends before grating.) Bake 1 hour.

Lemon Loaf Cake

This is the perfect solution for using up extra egg yolks. It uses only the egg yolks, has a rich, tender crumb. Pair it with ice cream, gelato or fruit.

Ingredients

(Prep time: 20 minutes at 350 degrees; Bake 50 minutes). Double the ingredients if you use a bundt pan. If you use a 9 x 5 x 3 loaf pan:

6 tbsp butter or margarine

1/2 cup plus 2 tbsp sugar

4 egg yokes (1/3 cup)

1-1/4 cups of cake flour>

1-1/2 tsp of baking powder

Dash of salt

1/3 cup milk

1/2 tsp of vanilla

1/4 tsp of lemon extract

Grated peel of 1 lemon

1/2 tsp lemon juice

Powdered sugar

Fresh Fruit for Garnish

Directions

Cream butter and gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy. Beat egg yolks in a separate bowl until light and lemon colored. Blend into the creamed butter mixture.

In a separate bowl, sift together dry ingredients (flour, baking powder and salt), alternately adding milk and beating well after each addition. Add the vanilla, lemon extract, lemon peel and lemon juice. Again, blend ingredients well.

Pour into a well-greased, floured loaf pan and bake at 350 for 50 minutes. Use a knife or cake tester to test if done. Dust top with powdered sugar, fresh fruit. Enjoy!

Peanut Butter Bread

Ingredients

(Prep time: 30 minutes; Bake @ 350 degrees)

2 cups - all purpose flour

1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1-1/4 teaspoons salt

1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

3/4 cup of firmly packed light brown sugar (Domino's or your favorite kind

3/4 cup of smooth or chunky peanut butter

1/4 cup of honey

1/4 cup (half stick) softened butter

2 large eggs

1-1/4 cup of whole or low-fat milk

optional: 1 cup of roasted peanuts

Directions

Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and baking soda. Beat the peanut butter and butter at medium speed until smooth; add the brown sugar and beat until smooth and light. Add the honey and eggs, 1 at a time; pour milk in a thin stream into the mixture.

Add flour mixture in at low speed until combined. Add in extra peanuts, if desired. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes, then reduce oven temp to 325 because this bread burns easily. Cover with foil to prevent overbrowning if necessary. Bake for 30 minutes more until done, testing with a knife.

Chocolate Chip-Banana Bread

Ingredients

(Prep time: 20 minutes; Bake at 350 degrees for 70-75 minutes)

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

3/4 cup granulated sugar

2 teaspoons baking powder

1/2 teaspoon salt

2 large, ripe mashed bananas

1/3 cup vegetable oil

2 large eggs

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions

Preheat oven to 350; grease loaf (8-1/2 x 4-1/2) pan. Dust it with flour and tap out excess.

Mix together flour, chocolate chips, sugar, baking powder and salt. Stir together mashed bananas, oil, eggs and vanilla in a separate bowl. Make a well in the center of the flour mixture and add the banana mixture in the center, tossing with a fork until dry ingredients are just moistened. Don't overmix. Spoon batter in prepared pan, smoothing the top.

Bake loaf for 70-75 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Loosen the loaf from the pan by running a metal spatula around the sides.

If you use a 9 x 5 loaf pan, reduce baking time to 60 minutes.
